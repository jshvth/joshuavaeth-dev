# Contact

## Summary
The footer focuses on direct contact with a large email link and optional social links.

## Highlights
- Prominent email address with hover underline expansion.
- Social icons for GitHub, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Buy Me a Coffee.
- Lightweight copyright line with current year.

## Implementation Notes
- Component: `src/components/Footer.jsx`

## Behavior
- Email hover expands a line for emphasis.
- Social labels hide on very small screens to keep the layout clean.
